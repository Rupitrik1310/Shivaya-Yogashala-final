200 Hour Yoga Teacher Training in Rishikesh

Shivaya Yogashala offers an authentic 200 Hour Multi-Style Yoga Teacher Training in Rishikesh, the yoga capital of the world. This foundational course covers Hatha Yoga, Ashtanga Yoga, pranayama, meditation, anatomy, and yoga philosophy.

Course Highlights

  • Hatha & Ashtanga Yoga Practice
  • Pranayama and Breathwork
  • Meditation Techniques
  • Yoga Philosophy and History
  • Teaching Methodology

Why Choose Rishikesh

Rishikesh is known as the spiritual home of yoga. Training here connects you to traditional yogic roots, Himalayan energy, and a supportive spiritual environment.
